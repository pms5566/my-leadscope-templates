How We Work

Our Proven 3-Step Process

From your first call to your final walkthrough, every GreenLawn installation follows a precise, time-tested process that guarantees a flawless result — every single time.

01

Base Preparation

We begin with full excavation of existing grass and soil to the proper depth, then install a commercial-grade weed barrier and a 3-inch crushed decomposed granite base — the foundation for a perfectly level, long-lasting lawn.

Excavation Weed Barrier Base Grading
02

Precision Installation

Our certified installers roll out premium turf, make invisible seam cuts, and secure the perimeter with galvanized nails and bender board edging. Every blade is oriented for a natural, consistent look across your entire yard.

Turf Layout Invisible Seams Perimeter Secure
03

Infill & Final Finish

We power-brush the turf to stand every blade upright, then apply the appropriate infill — crumb rubber, ZeoFill, or silica sand — to give your lawn that full, realistic look. A final walkthrough confirms your 100% satisfaction.

Common Questions

Everything You Need
to Know

Straight answers from Dallas's most experienced artificial turf team.

How does dog urine drain through the turf?
Our pet turf systems use a double-coated, fully permeable backing that drains at a rate of 30 inches per hour — far exceeding what any Texas rainstorm or dog can produce. Urine passes straight through the turf into the crushed stone base below, where it disperses harmlessly into the ground. Combined with our ZeoFill antimicrobial infill, your yard will stay odor-free and hygienic year-round, with no rinsing required.
Does artificial turf get too hot in Dallas summers?
This is the #1 question we get, and it's a valid one for Texas. Standard turf can get warm in direct midday sun. That's why all our residential installations use HeatBlock Cool-Grass infill — a light-reflective, moisture-retaining silica blend that keeps turf surface temperatures up to 40°F cooler than competing products. Our turf is also certified UV-stabilized so it won't fade, melt, or yellow even after 15+ years of Texas sun.
What is the lifespan of artificial turf?
Every GreenLawn installation comes with a 15-year manufacturer warranty covering fade, drainage failure, and fiber breakdown. In real-world conditions, our turf routinely lasts 20 to 25 years with minimal upkeep — just an occasional rinse and brush. Compare that to the ongoing cost of natural grass: mowing, fertilizing, aeration, overseeding, pest control, and irrigation. The math is clear.
How long does a typical installation take?
Most residential installations — including full front and back yard projects up to 3,000 sq ft — are completed in 1 to 2 days. Larger estates or complex putting greens with custom contouring may take 3-4 days. We provide a firm timeline before any work begins, and our crews work efficiently to minimize disruption to your home.
Will artificial turf increase my home's value?
Yes — consistently. In the DFW market, professional artificial turf installation has been shown to increase home resale value by 5-10% while dramatically improving curb appeal. Buyers love the low-maintenance lifestyle, and in water-scarce Texas, a turf lawn is increasingly seen as a smart, responsible upgrade. Custom putting greens add a premium entertainment value that's hard to match.
