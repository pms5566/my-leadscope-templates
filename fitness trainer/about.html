About Ashish

Hi, I'm Ashish Satarkar

A REPs certified personal fitness trainer with 3+ years of hands-on coaching experience. I started FitItUp because I believe fitness should be accessible to everyone, whether you have a gym, a home, or just 30 minutes a day.

I've worked with 500+ clients across India, helping them lose fat, build strength, improve their lifestyle, and most importantly, stay consistent. My approach is simple: understand your life first, then build a plan that fits it.

I specialise in home workouts, calisthenics, personalised diet plans, online e-coaching, and kids fitness training. If you're ready to take your first step, I'm here to walk with you.
