Our Heritage

Crafted with Soul in the Hills of Kathmandu

Preeti Agrawal Jewelry blends ancient spiritual methodologies with luxurious modern craftsmanship. Born in the heart of Kathmandu, Nepal, our brand selects rare crystals and healing gemstones, honoring their geological origins and esoteric qualities.

Every piece is hand-wrought by heritage Nepalese Newar silversmiths, keeping a historic, centuries-old art form alive. We transform minerals into fine high-end jewelry that is as spiritually balancing as it is physically breathtaking.
