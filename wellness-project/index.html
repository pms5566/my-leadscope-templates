Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've compiled details to help clarify how online premium coaching and training works.

How does online fitness & nutrition coaching work?

Upon applying and selecting your package, we schedule a deep-dive strategy video call. We analyze your history, gym accessibility, dietary needs, and physiological markers. We load your routines and macros into our private training app. You log workouts, check in weekly with photos/measurements, and receive personalized video feedback and plan adjustments.

Do I need access to a full commercial gym?

Absolutely not. We write custom protocols tailored specifically to your environment. Whether you have access to a five-star luxury gym, a basic hotel gym during business travel, or simply a pair of dumbbells at home, we build the training program to maximize your available resources.

What makes Aura Fit Co. coaching different from other programs?

Most programs offer static, generic PDF templates. Aura Fit Co. provides hyper-customized bio-individual design. Guided by a certified WFF Bikini Pro, we don't just assign exercises; we monitor biomechanical form, adjust macro plans based on hormonal response, and structure stress-management systems to support your nervous system.

Is there a minimum commitment period?

To build lasting neural pathways and physiological adaptation, we recommend a minimum commitment of 12 weeks. Real, permanent lifestyle change takes time, and our best case transformations are achieved within 12 to 24 weeks of consistency.
